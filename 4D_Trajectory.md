# Terms & Abbreviations
## RBT 
reference business trajectory, the trajectory the airspace user agrres to fly

## CTA 
controlled time of arrival 

## TBO
trajectory based operations
the concept of improving throughput, flight efficiency, flight times, and schedule predictability through better prediction and coordination of aircraft trajectories;

https://csaob.larc.nasa.gov/projects/trajectory-based-operations/

## SWIM 
system wide information management is an advanced technology program designed to facilitate greater sharing of Air Traffic Management (ATM) system information, such as airport operational status, weather information, flight data, status of special use airspace, etc.
