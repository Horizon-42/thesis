# 与 KRDU 相似、且地形/地理差异较大的美国机场推荐

## 筛选基准 / Selection baseline

KRDU 在 2024 年旅客量约为 1,550 万人次，是一个中等偏大的美国非超级枢纽机场。  
KRDU handled about 15.5 million passengers in 2024, making it a medium-to-large U.S. airport rather than a mega-hub.

本推荐优先选择年旅客量约 500 万至 1,200 万人次、机场运行复杂度适中、且周边地形差异明显的机场。  
This recommendation prioritizes airports with roughly 5–12 million annual passengers, moderate operational complexity, and distinctive surrounding terrain.

---

## 1. KSJC — San José Mineta International Airport / 圣何塞米内塔国际机场

KSJC 位于加州硅谷，2024 年旅客量接近 1,200 万人次，规模低于 KRDU 但仍属于较活跃的中型机场。  
KSJC is located in Silicon Valley, California, and handled nearly 12 million passengers in 2024, making it smaller than KRDU but still a busy mid-sized airport.

它适合研究山湾复合地形下的进近航迹，因为机场靠近旧金山湾、Santa Cruz Mountains 和 Diablo Range。  
It is suitable for studying approach trajectories in mixed bay-and-mountain terrain because the airport is close to San Francisco Bay, the Santa Cruz Mountains, and the Diablo Range.

KSJC 的进近航迹可能受到湾区多机场环境影响，包括 KSFO 和 KOAK 的邻近空域。  
KSJC approach trajectories may also be affected by the multi-airport Bay Area environment, including nearby KSFO and KOAK airspace.

推荐用途：山地边界、海湾平原、多机场终端区交互。  
Recommended use: mountain boundaries, bay plains, and multi-airport terminal-area interactions.

---

## 2. KSAT — San Antonio International Airport / 圣安东尼奥国际机场

KSAT 位于德克萨斯州中南部，2024 年旅客量约 1,109 万人次，和 KRDU 同属中等偏大机场。  
KSAT is in south-central Texas and handled about 11.09 million passengers in 2024, placing it in a similar medium-large category to KRDU.

它的地形环境不同于 KRDU，机场周边包含城市平原、缓丘和 Texas Hill Country 过渡地带。  
Its terrain environment differs from KRDU because the airport sits near urban plains, rolling terrain, and the transition toward the Texas Hill Country.

KSAT 适合研究较平坦地形与缓丘地形混合条件下的机场进近模型。  
KSAT is useful for approach modeling under mixed flat-terrain and rolling-hill conditions.

推荐用途：南部内陆机场、城市热岛背景、低起伏地形进近。  
Recommended use: southern inland airport, urban-background operations, and low-relief terrain approaches.

---

## 3. KCLE — Cleveland Hopkins International Airport / 克利夫兰霍普金斯国际机场

KCLE 位于俄亥俄州北部，2024 年旅客量约 1,017 万人次，规模明显接近 KRDU。  
KCLE is in northern Ohio and handled about 10.17 million passengers in 2024, making it clearly comparable in scale to KRDU.

它靠近 Lake Erie，适合研究湖岸、低云、风场变化和内陆平原交界处的进近航迹。  
It is close to Lake Erie, making it useful for studying approach trajectories near lake-shore terrain, low-cloud regimes, wind shifts, and inland plains.

KCLE 的地形起伏不如西部机场强，但湖岸气象和城市地形背景具有代表性。  
KCLE has less terrain relief than western airports, but its lake-shore meteorology and urban-terrain background are representative.

推荐用途：五大湖区域、湖岸进近、低起伏城市机场。  
Recommended use: Great Lakes region, lake-shore approaches, and low-relief urban airports.

---

## 4. KPIT — Pittsburgh International Airport / 匹兹堡国际机场

KPIT 位于宾夕法尼亚州西部，2024 年旅客量约 995 万人次，规模与 KRDU 接近。  
KPIT is in western Pennsylvania and handled about 9.95 million passengers in 2024, making it comparable to KRDU.

它周边属于 Appalachian Plateau 及河谷丘陵地形，比 KRDU 周边地形更复杂。  
Its surroundings include Appalachian Plateau and river-valley hill terrain, which is more topographically complex than the area around KRDU.

KPIT 适合研究丘陵、河谷和非规则地形对进近航迹高度剖面的影响。  
KPIT is suitable for studying how hills, river valleys, and irregular terrain affect vertical profiles during approach.

推荐用途：丘陵机场、河谷地形、复杂地形下的下降轨迹建模。  
Recommended use: hilly airport environment, river-valley terrain, and descent-profile modeling over complex terrain.

---

## 5. KCVG — Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport / 辛辛那提-北肯塔基国际机场

KCVG 位于肯塔基州北部、辛辛那提都市圈南侧，2024 年旅客量超过 920 万人次。  
KCVG is in northern Kentucky, south of the Cincinnati metro area, and handled more than 9.2 million passengers in 2024.

它兼具客运和货运活动，适合研究混合交通流下的机场进近航迹预测。  
It has both passenger and cargo activity, making it useful for approach-trajectory prediction under mixed traffic flows.

KCVG 周边包含 Ohio River Valley 和起伏丘陵，与 KRDU 的低起伏皮埃蒙特地形形成对比。  
The area around KCVG includes the Ohio River Valley and rolling hills, contrasting with KRDU’s lower-relief Piedmont terrain.

推荐用途：客货混合机场、河谷丘陵、夜间货运交通影响。  
Recommended use: mixed passenger-cargo airport, river-valley hills, and nighttime cargo-traffic effects.

---

## 6. KABQ — Albuquerque International Sunport / 阿尔伯克基国际机场

KABQ 位于新墨西哥州高海拔沙漠地区，2025 年旅客量约 531 万人次，规模小于 KRDU 但仍适合补充样本。  
KABQ is in the high-desert region of New Mexico and handled about 5.31 million passengers in 2025, smaller than KRDU but still useful as a supplemental sample.

它的最大价值是地形差异极强，机场靠近 Sandia Mountains，高海拔、山地和沙漠环境明显。  
Its main value is strong terrain contrast: the airport is near the Sandia Mountains, with high-elevation, mountainous, and desert conditions.

KABQ 适合用于检验模型在高海拔机场和强地形边界条件下的泛化能力。  
KABQ is useful for testing model generalization at high-elevation airports with strong terrain boundaries.

推荐用途：高海拔机场、山地/沙漠地形、强地形约束进近。  
Recommended use: high-elevation airport, mountain/desert terrain, and terrain-constrained approaches.

---

## 建议组合 / Suggested airport set

如果只选 3 个机场，建议选择 KSJC、KPIT 和 KABQ。  
If selecting only three airports, choose KSJC, KPIT, and KABQ.

这个组合覆盖西海岸山湾地形、东部丘陵河谷地形和西南高海拔沙漠山地。  
This set covers West Coast bay-and-mountain terrain, eastern hill-and-river-valley terrain, and southwestern high-desert mountain terrain.

如果选 5 个机场，建议选择 KSJC、KSAT、KCLE、KPIT 和 KABQ。  
If selecting five airports, choose KSJC, KSAT, KCLE, KPIT, and KABQ.

这个组合在流量规模、气候、地形和地理位置上比单一区域机场更有多样性。  
This set provides better diversity in traffic scale, climate, terrain, and geography than airports from a single region.

如果你希望加入货运和夜间运行特征，可以把 KCVG 替换或加入第五个机场。  
If you want cargo and nighttime-operation characteristics, KCVG can replace one airport or be added as the fifth airport.
